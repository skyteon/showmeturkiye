Canakkale

Overview

Places to Visit in Canakkale

A

Canakkale Martyrs' Memorial

Memorial with the name of every soldier who died in the war. On March 18, memorial program held for soldiers. The 41.70-meter-high monument erected Aug. 21, 1960, consists of four square columns 7.5-meter wide with 10-meter space topped by concrete slab of 25 by 25 meters. Inscription features verses from Turkish national anthem by Mehmet Akif Ersoy. War Museum beneath the memorial contains historical artifacts, personal items like cutlery, false teeth, dress buttons, belt buckles, sniper shields, and photographs from battlefield illustrating Battle of Gallipoli against Allied powers.

B

Kilitbahir Castle

Fatih Sultan Mehmet had two castles built in narrowest part of Dardanelles to protect strait from enemy attack. All ships entering and leaving Bosphorus were stopped and checked. Castle positioned in narrowest part for effective cannon shots. Since it's parallel with Cimenlik Castle in Canakkale, mutual cannon fires intersect making it extremely difficult to pass through with no dead area where ships can pass unharmed.

C

Canakkale Clock Tower

Clock tower located in city center. Built in 1896, the tower is 20 meters high and has five floors.

D

Troy Horse

Famous from Greek mythology and Troy movie about Trojan prince Paris and Helen Queen of Sparta. After 10-year war, soldiers offered Trojan horse as gift with white flag to end war, but army was hidden inside huge horse to conquer castle from inside. Original Troy Horse from movie is located in Canakkale. The 12.4-meter-long Trojan Horse brought from Japan in 2004 was put on display in Canakkale.

E

Aynalı Çarşı

Covered market located in Canakkale. Built in 1890 by Eliyau Hallio, one of notables of city's Jewish community. You can find anything in this bazaar which takes place in folk songs.

F

Maritime Museum

Historical sailing ships, cannons, and other heavy weapons exhibited in museum. Has open and closed areas inside the park.

G

Gallipoli Peninsula Historical National Park

Historical place of great importance. Tours available to guide visitors through the historical significance.

H

Islands: Bozcaada, Gökçeada, Tavşan Island

Canakkale has many beautiful islands. Bozcaada known for wineries and beaches. Gökçeada is Turkey's largest island. Tavşan Island for nature lovers.

Why Visit Canakkale?

Çanakkale guards the Dardanelles, where Europe and Asia come within swimming distance and every current carries history. Troy sits just south of the city, still revealing secrets after 3,000 years of excavation. Gallipoli remembers a different war, with cemeteries and memorials scattered across windswept hills that drop into the sea. The city itself stays modest and welcoming, with fish restaurants lining the waterfront and ferries crossing to Asia every twenty minutes. You can touch three millennia of history in a single day here, then eat fresh calamari while watching the sun set over the strait.

Food & Cuisine

Cheese Halva

Has unsalted cheese in it. Also has special technique to make it.

Salted Sardines

Takes long time to make, maybe a month or more. This dish, which takes great effort to make, is a flavor you can find almost anywhere in the city.

Çırpma

With appearance resembling spinach pastry, obtained by adding spinach and onion to dough prepared with only flour and water and baking the resulting mixture. Do not forget to taste if you visit Canakkale.

Activities & Experiences

Ayazma Waterfall

Located within borders of Bayramiç at foot of Kaz Mountains. Camping area stands out as haunt for adrenaline sports enthusiasts. Make exciting jumps into ice-cold water and swim. Overnight camping activities available to enjoy green tones of Kaz Mountains.

Paragliding

Kaz mountains has beautiful view from top. You can reach altitude of 2500 meters in Yenice, Kaz Mountains.

Boat Tour

Find boat tours to islands. Some tours have lunch included. Better to go on tours to the island.

Diving

Many diving points within Canakkale borders. Assos and Bozcaada surfaced with high underwater visibility full of natural beauties. You can find tours to dive with people. See ships under water from war times.

Near Places to Visit

Balikesir: Canakkale is 3 hours away from Balikesir. Special beauty even on road with many places to capture.
