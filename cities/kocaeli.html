Kocaeli

Overview

Places to Visit in Kocaeli

A

Kartepe

Kartepe dazzles with its natural beauty. Many activities available in this mountain region combining skiing in winter with hiking and nature in summer.

B

Serindere Canyon

Has 6 waterfalls between 10 and 20 meters in height. Most challenging canyon of Kocaeli and the region. Best place to trek in Türkiye. City tours available to go to canyon with people who like to trek. The water is really cold so wrap up!

C

Pink Rocks

Must-see place with different geological structures. In addition to prehistoric finds, has been used as quarry since Antiquity. Pink rocks soft in water become hard after removed. Due to these features, they were cut into rectangles during Ottoman Period and transported to Istanbul by sea for use in architecture. Great view for drone photography!

D

Izmit Gulf

Beautiful gulf bringing the sea right into the city. Popular for waterfront walks, seafood restaurants, and sunset views.

E

Izmit Clock Tower

Historic landmark in Izmit city center. Symbol of the city with its distinctive architecture.

Why Visit Kocaeli?

Kocaeli sits between Istanbul and Ankara, often overlooked but worth the stop. Izmit Gulf brings the sea right into the city, and Kartepe Mountain offers skiing in winter and highland hiking in summer. The region balances industrial energy with surprising pockets of nature: waterfalls hidden in forests, lakeside picnic spots where locals escape on weekends, and coastal paths perfect for sunset walks. It's Turkey's productive heartland that somehow still makes time for tea gardens and fresh fish restaurants.

Food & Cuisine

Stuffed Turkey

Indispensable dish of New Year's Eve and special occasions in Kocaeli. Prepared with stuffed rice containing peeled roasted chestnuts, currants, and turkey liver. Possible to taste this wonderful dish in Izmit.

Cızlama

Delicacy you can eat at any time of day. Unlike pancakes, yeast is put into it. Made by cooking on sheet metal in many villages of Kocaeli.

Activities & Experiences

Skiing and Winter Sports

Kartepe offers skiing and winter sports activities. Equipment rental and ski schools available.

Trekking and Canyon Exploration

Serindere Canyon provides challenging trekking with 6 waterfalls. Best trekking destination in region.

Photography and Nature

Pink Rocks offer unique geological formations perfect for photography, especially drone shots.

Near Places to Visit

Istanbul: Close to Kocaeli, takes one and a half hours by car. Largest city in Türkiye. You may find Istanbul tours from Kocaeli.

Düzce: One and a half hours from Kocaeli province. Has beaches and waterfalls.
