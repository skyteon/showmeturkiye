Erzurum

Overview

Places to Visit in Erzurum

A

Erzurum Grand Mosque & Double Minaret Madrasah

Erzurum Grand Mosque is the oldest and biggest mosque in Erzurum. Historical monuments are worthy in terms of architecture containing structures of different periods. The first Turkish principality established in Anatolia after Battle of Manzikert was Saltuklu Principality, founded in Erzurum as capital. The city still preserves its historical texture. The Double Minaret Madrasah is the largest in Anatolia, built in second half of 13th century (Seljuk period), initially used as theological school. After years it became ruin and was repaired by Murad IV and used as armory. After 1942, it became museum and exhibition hall.

B

Three Tombs

Located about 300 meters south of The Double Minaret Madrasah. Three Tombs is a mausoleum, not an actual tomb.

C

Erzurum Castle

The Castle of Erzurum was built about 2,500 years ago by the Urartians who dominated the region. The first version of the inner castle, which still exists today, was built by Byzantine Emperor Theodosius in 415.

D

Narman Fairy Chimneys

When you think of Fairy Chimneys, Cappadocia comes to mind first. But there are fairy chimneys in many cities of Turkey. Narman Fairy Chimneys is a hidden treasure in Türkiye with its magical view, also known as the "Land of the Red Fairies".

E

Tortum Waterfall

The Tortum Waterfall is the tallest in Turkey. Since the end of the Tortum Dam and Hydroelectric Plant in 1960, water drawn from Tortum Lake through channels allowed to rush down into turbines. The waterfall is fed only from surplus water and thus now functions only for short period during May and June when water level of the lake is exceptionally high.

F

Palandöken Ski Resort

The eastern part of Türkiye has many ski resorts because of the highlands. Erzurum Palandöken has abundant snow in the region and upright ski-runs. You can find tours from city center to Palandöken. You can also rent materials such as snowboard, ski, helmet, etc.

Why Visit Erzurum?

Erzurum sits at nearly 2,000 meters altitude, where winter dominates the calendar and locals have adapted accordingly. Palandöken Mountain offers some of Turkey's best skiing without the European price tags, and the city itself preserves Seljuk architecture that most tourists skip on their way to warmer destinations. Cağ kebab tastes different here, cooked over wood fires that fight off the mountain cold. Summer brings wildflowers to alpine meadows and temperatures perfect for hiking, but this city really comes alive when snow starts falling.

Food & Cuisine

Cağ Kebab

Cağ Kebab is the most popular traditional food in Erzurum. It is made by resting the meat for 2 days but when you start eating it will finish immediately.

Asotu Soup

Asotu soup, one of the traditional soups of Erzurum cuisine, is a soup that is usually cooked at home and served to guests. It takes its name from its main ingredient, asortu. This herb is also known as a natural protector against many diseases.

Kadayıf Dolması

Kadayıf dolması, a dessert unique to Erzurum, used to be made at home only during Ramadan. Later, it was taken out of the home kitchen, its syrup and shape were changed and it became a dessert served in restaurants.

Activities & Experiences

Skiing at Palandöken

Erzurum Palandöken has abundant snow and upright ski-runs. Tours available from city center. Equipment rental available including snowboards, skis, and helmets.

Jeep Safari

There are interesting routes for jeep safari in Erzurum which draws attention with its natural beauties and interesting landforms.

Paragliding

Erzurum, which has rugged and high mountainous-hilly terrain, is suitable for paragliding. Suitable points throughout the province, especially around Palandöken Mountains and Tortum Lake.

Rafting

Coruh River, one of the rivers where rafting can be done best in the world, passes through the İspir district.

Near Places to Visit

Kars: Almost 2 and a half hours away from Erzurum. Kars is the last station of the famous Eastern Express train journey.

Erzincan: 2 and a half hours away from Erzurum with different beauty standards.
