Rize

Overview

Places to Visit in Rize

A

Huser Plateau

Huser Plateau is the best place you can watch the sunrise and the sunset. You can also see the clouds from the top of the mountain. You can find a swing and have beautiful memories.

B

Kaçkar Mountains National Park

Kaçkar Mountains is the highest point of the Black Sea. It is a very popular place for mountaineering because of its nature. Groups come to the region from Artvin to Rize. The Altiparmak mountain range is the eastern part of the Kaçkar Mountains. The highest peak is the Duatepe peak with 3488m. Palovit Waterfall is located in the Kaçkar Mountains national park at 15 meters high. It takes about one hour of driving, but in beautiful forest. You may also camp inside the park!

C

Zilkale (Castle of Rize)

Turkey has so many castles in almost every region but the castle of Rize has a different view than other castles. Although the history of the castle is not known exactly, it is known that the inner castle part was rebuilt during the Justinian period. Black Sea sides are so green because of its nature that makes wonderful view for the castle.

D

Pokut Plateau

It is located between the valleys formed by the Fırtına Stream and Hala Stream at an altitude of 2032 meters. It is usually foggy but still a beautiful mountain village. If you can be fast, you can catch the foggy mountain view from the top of the mountain. Do not come here with small cars, you need a 4 wheel drive car!

E

Fırtına River

Fırtına River is one of the main water streams of Rize Province in the eastern Black Sea Region of Turkey. Its name is Turkish for "stormwater". There are more than 20 well-preserved Ottoman-era bridges over the Fırtına stream.

Why Visit Rize?

Rize blankets itself in tea plantations that climb from the Black Sea to mountain peaks disappearing into cloud cover. This is where Turkey grows its tea, and you taste the difference in every glass served roadside. The Fırtına Valley cuts through the province with rivers cold enough to make you gasp and bridges older than most countries. Between the coast and the highlands you'll find a version of Turkey that runs on its own clock, where weather changes hourly and locals still measure distances in tea breaks.

Food & Cuisine

Çay (Tea)

Çay is the most important drink in Turkey. Rize is the main place for Turkish tea. You should visit the garden of tea in Rize. Don't forget to buy tea for your loved ones!

Muhlama

Muhlama is a regional meal of the Black Sea region of Turkey, made with corn meal and special cheese. Muhlama is a special meal for breakfast.

Rize Pita

Rize Pita is served uncut because the taste of the pita can change if it's cut. It has been made in the region for many years. The naturalness of the materials used, the production technique, and the skill of the masters have reached the present day.

Activities & Experiences

Rafting

The nature of the Black Sea region is the most beautiful place for rafting. Rize, which has rivers with high flow due to its mountainous structure, is the first stop for extreme sports lovers. Fırtına River is one of the best locations in the city.

Trekking and Mountaineering

The City of Rize has so many options for trekking trails. Here is the list of the best trekking trails in Rize:

• Trans Kaçkar (24.85 miles)

• Koçduzu - Derebaşı Trail - Altiparmak - Ambar Lake - Eğrisu Lake - Eğrisu Highland - Toptanca Highland - Didi Zuga - Koçduzu (15.5 miles)

• Kavrun - Büyük Deniz Lake - Meterez - Serdar Mountain pass - Mezovit - Kavrun (8.28 miles)

Near Places to Visit

Trabzon: Rize is 1 hour away from Trabzon. You may find tours from Rize to Trabzon Province. Its nature is very similar to Rize but its beauty is worth to be seen.
