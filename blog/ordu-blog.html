Discovering Ordu: A Traveler's Guide to Must-Visit Places and Experiences

June 11, 2023

Want to experience Black Sea region spirit to the fullest? You're in for a treat! Nestled along the mesmerizing Black Sea coast, Ordu offers an irresistible blend of natural wonders, rich history, and warm hospitality. Whether you're an adventure seeker, a history buff, or simply seeking a relaxing getaway, Ordu has something to offer every traveler.

Ordu Ulugol Nature Park

This park is characterized by its mesmerizing Uluğöl Lake, which is surrounded by lush forests, rolling hills, and stunning mountain ranges. The serene lake, with its crystal-clear waters, serves as the centerpiece of this picturesque park. It is approximately 80 kilometers away from Ordu city center. After a 2-hour journey from the city center, you will find yourself in front of the captivating view of Ulugol Lake.

Take a walk around the lake, embrace the park's serene atmosphere by setting up camp or enjoying a delightful picnic amidst nature's embrace. The designated tent and van camping areas provide the perfect opportunity to spend quality time with friends and family while basking in the park's beauty. Bungalows are also available for accommodation in the park.

The beauty of Ordu Uluğöl Nature Park can be experienced throughout the year. However, the months between spring (April-May) and autumn (September-October) are considered ideal, with pleasant temperatures and the added bonus of colorful foliage during the autumn months.

Ohtamis Waterfall

Ohtamış Waterfall is situated near the village of Ohtamış, approximately 25 kilometers from the city center of Ordu. Nestled in a picturesque valley, the waterfall is surrounded by lush greenery and a tranquil atmosphere.

Prepare to be mesmerized by the beauty of the waterfall. Cascading down from a height of approximately 30 meters, the waterfall is a sight to behold. The rushing water creates a captivating spectacle. Take a moment to unwind and reflect in the tranquil atmosphere surrounding the waterfall. Breathe in the fresh air and let the serenity wash over you.

Persembe Plateau

One of the first destinations that come to mind when it comes to must-visit places in Ordu is Persembe Plateau. What makes this place a sight to behold and causes the number of visitors to increase day by day is its interesting meanders. These rivers, which look like winding roads, display such a beautiful image that it is impossible not to be amazed.

Thanks to the nature revived with the arrival of spring, a lush greenery covers the plateau. Especially the sheeps and lambs grazing in the meandering areas add beauty to the landscape.

If you want to add excitement to the beauty of the Persembe Plateau, don't miss the events held here in summer! Activities such as oil wrestling festivals, horse and off-road races, and football tournaments are worth experiencing in this heavenly place.

Unye Castle

Unye Castle is a historical gem that offers visitors a glimpse into the region's past. The castle dates back to the Byzantine era and has witnessed various civilizations and historical events over the centuries. Even in the architectural structure, it is possible to see different elements from the Byzantine, Genoese, and Ottoman periods.

As you explore the castle, you'll encounter remnants of walls, towers, and gateways that stand as a testament to its glorious past. The castle's vantage point gives you the chance to enjoy panoramic vistas, expansive Black Sea view and surrounding lush landscapes.

Cape Jason

Here is another destination that you should add to your Ordu travel plan with its picturesque coastal view. Cape Jason! This scenic cape offers breathtaking views of the sea and surrounding landscapes. With its rugged cliffs, pristine beaches, and crystal-clear waters, the cape provides a mesmerizing backdrop for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts.

If you're wondering what time of day is best to visit Cape Jason, sunset would be the perfect timing. Take in the sight of the endless horizon, watch the waves crash against the cliffs, and witness the enchanting colors of the sunset painting the sky.

Ordu Uzunkum Beach

Uzunkum Beach is known for its long stretch of sandy shoreline and its tranquil ambiance. Unwind on the sandy shore, bask in the sun's warmth, and listen to the gentle sound of waves crashing against the beach. It's the perfect place to escape the hustle and bustle of daily life and enjoy a tranquil beach experience.

The calm and crystal clear waters of Uzunkum Beach are ideal for swimming, but if it is not enough for you, Uzunkum Beach also provides opportunities for water sports such as jet skiing, paddleboarding and kayaking, adding a touch of adventure to your visit.

Ordu Boztepe

Situated atop a picturesque hill, this destination offers stunning panoramas, serene surroundings. From the hilltop, you can enjoy the mesmerizing natural beauties and fresh air while drinking your tea!

The cable car ride is also an experience in itself and provides a unique perspective of the area. Embark on an exciting cable car ride to reach the summit of Ordu Boztepe and enjoy the scenic journey as you glide above the lush greenery, taking in the beauty below.

Boztepe also does not forget to satisfy adventure seekers. It offers an exhilarating paragliding experience! Soar through the air and witness the stunning landscape from a bird's-eye view. The adrenaline rush and the panoramic vistas will make it a truly memorable adventure.
